DISEC

DISEC, the First Committee of the UN General Assembly, focuses on safeguarding international peace. It tackles major challenges such as nuclear weapons, arms regulation, and global or regional tensions. Delegates collaborate to propose resolutions that aim to reduce threats, promote stability, and encourage responsible international security practices.
