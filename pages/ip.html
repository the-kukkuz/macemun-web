IP

The International Press (IP) team documents every major moment of the MUN as it unfolds. They offer instant updates on committee sessions, debates, and diplomatic developments. Writers focus on accurate, unbiased reporting, while photographers highlight the visual aspects of the event. Together, they build a complete story of the discussions, decisions, and interactions that shape the conference.
