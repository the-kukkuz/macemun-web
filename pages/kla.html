KLA

The KLA committee at MACE MUN immerses delegates in the workings of Kerala’s state legislature. Participants take on the role of lawmakers, examining regional political concerns and legislative processes. Instead of global affairs, this committee focuses on state-level issues, offering a chance to understand Kerala’s governance and craft solutions for local socio-economic problems.
