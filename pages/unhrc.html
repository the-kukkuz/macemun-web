UNHRC

The UNHRC committee allows delegates to engage with urgent human rights crises around the world. Participants debate, negotiate, and draft policies to uphold justice and protect human dignity. The committee encourages delegates to take on the perspective of global advocates working to defend rights and create long-term humanitarian solutions.
